What is Pair Programming?

Pair programming is working with other people on a programming project. This is done in person or remotely. Pair programming is a commonly used development technique.

Why is pair proramming important to DBC?

Pair Progromming is important to DBC because a lot of programming involves group work. One benefit of pair programming is being able to have two sets of eyes on a problem and the ability to arrive at better solutions.

Compare driver and Navigator?

Driver: the person who has their hands on the keyboard and is familiar with all of the technical functions

Navigator: the person who has the wireframe, map, or process and who is telling the drivier what to do
